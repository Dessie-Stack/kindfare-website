Meet Mia

Your supportive AI coach, always here for you

KindFare™ is built around personalised coaching — a wellbeing app for iPhone with Mia, a friendly AI helper, at its centre. Mia keeps an eye on four areas of everyday wellbeing:

Cardiovascular

Managing heart health and blood pressure.

Musculoskeletal

Addressing joint and bone health.

Mental health

Supporting emotional wellbeing and resilience.

Nutritional wellbeing

Encouraging balanced diets and healthy choices.
